There are two options:

  • pom.xml: 
            
                org.mortbay.jetty
                maven-jetty-plugin
                6.1.10
                
                    ...
                    
                    
                        
                            jetty.port
                            8080
  • command prompt: mvn -Djetty.port=8090 jetty:run

The thing to remember is the command line value will override the value in pom.xml.
